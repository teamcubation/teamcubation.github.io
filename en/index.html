How do we do it?

01

Discovery

After assessing your methodologies, technological stack, and company culture, we evaluate your projects together to choose the most suitable option. If necessary, we assist in finding junior talent.

02

Execution

Over a 6-month period, we work alongside your junior devs to develop the software your company needs. This provides your new collaborators with the real experience of working on your teams.

03

Delivery

At the end of the sixth month, your company will have the software ready for implementation and a team of 3 experienced devs prepared to take on your next project. Congratulations! Your new source of specialized talent has successfully completed their first delivery.

We work in squads to turn beginners into ready-to-work talent.

Your juniors

PEP:

Programming Experience Platform.

The magic of our methodology lies in the data.

Our software is the differentiator and a great ally of our staff. It turns our training into a guided and measured process to enhance the development of each of your juniors and achieve their best version.

Integration into your workflow

PEP integrates with your main development tools like Jira, GitLab, Github, Slack, Discord, and more, to gather key data and optimize your team's performance.

Real-time insights

Observe your juniors' evolution with precise metrics on the code they develop, methodologies, and communication tools.

Intelligent alerts

Keep track of your juniors' performance with notifications that identify behaviors misaligned with expectations, essential to improve your team's quality and consistency of work.

Customizable tracking

Evaluate key aspects of your juniors' personal development through tailored actions and surveys that provide a human focus in the training process.
