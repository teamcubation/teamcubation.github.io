How do we do it?

01

Turnkey software development

We create customized solutions that meet your specific needs.

02

Training in action

Our juniors, who already have programming skills, accelerate the acquisition of experience in real software development through our proprietary methodology and technology, accompanied by our seniors.

03

Talent selection

We jointly choose the juniors that best fit your culture to be part of our team. At the end of the project, you can choose to hire the already trained juniors who meet your standards to permanently incorporate them into your team.

Technology and methodology to enhance your potential future collaborators.

Our leaders

Specialized and trained in your company's technology stack and methodologies, they will ensure that the software meets requirements with quality and will oversee the evolution of developers.

Your future developers

Guided by our seniors, they will gain real experience while developing their technical and interpersonal skills and integrating into your corporate culture.

PEP:

Programming Experience Platform.

The magic of our methodology is in the data.

While the team works, we collect and analyze concrete data to evaluate and improve the skills of our junior developers with the help of our Programming Experience Platform (PEP). This allows our seniors to support each one in a personalized way to ensure they align with the culture, methodologies, and technologies of your teams, helping them acquire the necessary experience to join them and be productive from day one.

Integration into your workflow

PEP connects with tools like GitHub, Jira, Slack, and Teams, facilitating both the software development process and tracking junior progress.

Real-time insights

Observe the evolution of developers with precise metrics on the code they develop, methodologies, and communication tools.

Intelligent alerts

Keep us informed about developer performance with notifications that identify behaviors that don't align with expectations, necessary to improve the quality and consistency of work and team.

Customizable monitoring

We evaluate key aspects of developers' personal development through customized actions and surveys that bring a human approach to the training process.

Meet your future collaborators

Through cognitive and behavioral tests, such as PDA (Personal Development Analysis), we get a clear view of your future collaborators' profiles to manage their talents, develop skills, and get their best version.

Emotional pulse

Integration with AI that automatically analyzes responses and detects the team's feelings and emotions to intervene timely when necessary, ensuring a positive and productive work environment.
