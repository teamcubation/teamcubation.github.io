The term referred to here as cookies should be understood in the broad sense, including other similar technologies such as local shared objects, flash cookies, web beacons, bugs, other tracking technologies, etc. 

  1. What are cookies?

A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that is downloaded to the hard drive of the computer, smartphone, tablet, etc. (hereinafter, the “Device") of the User when accessing the Website and consenting to the use of cookies. Through cookies, the Website can "remember" the User's actions or preferences over time.

  1. What are the types of cookies?

Strictly necessary: Also called "technical" or "functional", they are those that allow navigation through the Website, guarantee its correct functioning and the use of the different options or services that exist therein. These are cookies that allow, for example, controlling traffic and data communication, identifying the session, accessing restricted access parts, controlling fraud linked to the security of the service, using security elements during navigation, enabling dynamic content ( for example, loading animation of a text or image).

Strictly necessary cookies guarantee essential functions to use the Website. These are our own cookies, for example, to guarantee that if you log in as a registered user, your session remains active while browsing each and every one of the different sections of the website. The use of strictly necessary cookies on the Website is necessary without your consent. Therefore, strictly necessary cookies cannot be activated or deactivated individually, although you can deactivate cookies in your browser at any time (see how to deactivate them in Clauses 6 and 7, below in these Cookie Policies).

Configuration: are those that allow information to be remembered so that the User can access the service with certain characteristics that can differentiate their experience from that of other users, such as, for example, the language, the number of results to be displayed when the user performs a search, the appearance or content of the service depending on the type of browser through which the user accesses the service or the region from which the user accesses the service, etc.

Analytics or measurement: are those that allow the monitoring and analysis of the behavior of all the Users of the Website. The information collected through this type of cookies is used to measure the activity of the websites, application or platform, in order to introduce improvements based on the analysis of the usage data made by users of the Website.

Behavioral advertising: are those that store information on user behavior obtained through continuous observation of their browsing habits, which allows the development of a specific user profile to display advertising and personalized content based on it. This makes it possible to manage, in the most efficient way possible, the advertising spaces and content of the Website according to the specific user profile.

Geographic location: are those that allow your precise geographical location to fulfill several of the previous purposes, such as showing you advertising related to the place where you are or in the language of said place.

Depending on the entity that manages the cookies, they can be:

Own cookies: These are those sent from the Website, generally used to identify preferences regarding language and location or to offer the basic functions of the Website.

Third party cookies: These are those that are sent to the User's device from a computer or domain that is not managed by TEAMCUBATION, but by another entity that processes the data obtained through cookies.

The User may configure the Website if it does not support certain types of cookies or specific cookies through the cookie banner or the cookie control panel available. www.teamcubation.com/cookies.
